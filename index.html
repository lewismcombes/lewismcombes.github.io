Lewis Combes' website

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in Computation and Number Theory at the University of Sydney, working with John Voight and Geordie Williamson. Before that, I was a PhD student at the University of Sheffield, under the supervision of Haluk Şengün. My thesis can be viewed here.

I am particularly interested in automorphic forms (especially Bianchi modular forms), the cohomology of arithmetic groups, Selmer groups, and periods.
